Webmaster's Note: If you want to find web sites about China, there are lots. Go to one of the large search sites, such as Altavista, and enter "republic of china", in double quotes as shown, for a search criteria. Understand that you'll get info for both Taiwan, (Republic of China), and the PRC, (People's Republic of China). And if you find other sites that are interesting, let me know, and I'll add them to this list.

If you're looking for information on mailing lists, click here.

General Cultural Sites
Vancouver's Chinese Cultural Centre Lots of info on Chinese culture and info on events and courses here in Vancouver.
Main FCC Site. Parent organization's site with lots of information and lots more links.
Chinese Embassy in the USA. An excellent site full of info on Chinese culture, government, history, and the issues that face China today.
Chinese Business World. Site about doing business in China, travelling China, or simply finding out all kinds of info about China.
Leon Poon's Chinese History Site. This guy's nuts to take on something this big, but it's great.
Andrew Johnson's List of China Web Sites. A student's list of interesting sites that he's found.
Sinica's Site. Basically a book store, they claim to have over 1,000 books on China available to purchase online. If you click on the "Mainland, People's Republic of China" link, you'll also get a long list of links to other China related sites.
Lonely Planets China Guide. Lots of info and a cool map.
China The Beautiful. A lovely site maintained by Dr. Ming L. Pei, who's love of Chinese culture is enormous.
Chinese Community Forum. An interesting mix of news, culture and newsletter.
Mining Co.'s Cultural Sites. Commercial site with an excellent list of more resources.
Site for Buying Chinese Interactive CDs (Mandarin Courses, History, Art, etc.)
Zhongwen.Com. Chinese Characters and culture, history of characters, online dictionary
China Sprout. A fun site that contains lots of cultural stuff for sail, such as music, clothing and art, and resources on China adoption.
Asia For Kids. An excellent company that sells Asian stuff, (such as Chinese dolls, instead of the usual kind with blonde hair and blue eyes,) for kids. Not cheap, but cool.
China Guide - Collection of culture and retail. Information on acupuncture, Chinese medicine, education, entertainment.
I Love China. Chinese magazines and newspapers, info on Chinese churches, organizations, and culture.
Mandarin Tools. Dictionary, (character lookup!) Flashcards, all kind of great stuff on Mandarin.
One Hundred Famous Chinese Women
My Home Town contains information on just about every major city in China.
Bear Children's Choir of Hong Kong. CDs from one of the premier Chinese Children's Choirs
Child Book. Lots of Chinese stories, music and videos for kids for sale.
Chinese Tapes. Great site with lots of cultural tapes for kids, movies, music, DVDs. Disney movies in Mandarin!
Chinese Canadian Photo Exhibit. An interesting photo and text display of Chinese Canadian lives.

Chinese New Year Sites
Enchanted Learning's Chinese New Year for Children. Lots of great crafts and information.
Chinese New Year Site maintained by a U Vic faculty member. Excellent info on what to do on all 15 days of New year, recipes, superstitions, taboos, and decorations.
Chinese New Year More info on the festival, and the ability to send electronic cards.
Chinese New Year More general info, recipes, info on the Chinese Zodiac, and the symbols of New Year.
Another List of Links Lots more stuff on New Year.

Dragon Boat Festival Sites
Vancouver Dragonboat Festival. The official web site for our own Dragonboat festival.
Mining Co. Site Commercial site with a good synopsis of the festival and its history.

Mining Co.'s Mooncake Site. Description, the legend, and at the bottom, four recipes.

Celebrating the Mid-Autumn Moon Festival
Chinapage's Moon Festival Links
Family Culture's Moon Festival Site, with lots of links to other sites.
