FAMILIES WITH CHILDREN ADOPTED IN CHINA -
BRITISH COLUMBIA CHAPTER
(FCC BC)

FCC BC is an affiliation of families in British Columbia with children adopted in China. FCC BC is affiliated with FCC's in other parts of North America and elsewhere and with the Adoptive Parents Association of British Columbia (APABC).

Please print out this form and mail it along with a cheque for $15 (in Canadian funds or equivalent in US funds after exchange) to:

FCC BC Membership - Peter Gibson
218 W. 23rd Ave.
Vancouver, B.C. V5Y 2H3

Fees are for 2001 calendar year. A summary accounting of sources and uses of FCC BC funds will appear in all future issues of Friends of the Family, FCC BC's newsletter.
