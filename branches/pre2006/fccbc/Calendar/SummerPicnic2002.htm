FCC SUMMER PICNIC

SUNDAY, JULY 7, 2002 -- 11 a.m. to 3 p.m.
RYALL PARK PICNIC SHELTER
QUEENSBOROUGH COMMUNITY CENTRE, NEW WESTMINSTER

Come join in the fun at our summer picnic. The park has a waterpark and a children's play area, with washroom facilities in the community centre. The FCC group has booked the covered picnic shelter at the south end of the park --- look for our banner and register at the shelter. $5.00 per FCC family; $8.00 for non FCC member families.

"Mulan", a children's entertainer, will be coming for the children, from 11:30 am until 2:30 pm. She'll be based at the picnic shelter, and will be face-painting, doing balloon animals, and in the afternoon, a short magic show. 

Bring your own food and beverages, beachchairs, sunscreen, bathing suits, sunglasses, towels -- did I forget anything??

Contact Heather McDonald (604) 275-3190, if you have any questions. Hope to see a good turn-out!
