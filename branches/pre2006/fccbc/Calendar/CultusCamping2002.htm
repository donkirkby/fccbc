CAMPING WEEKEND AT CULTAS LAKE ON SATURDAY AUGUST 17

Would you and your family be interested in joining us in a FCC Camping weekend at Cultus Lake (near Abbotsford and Chilliwack) that promises fun and adventures for children and parents alike? We suggest Entrance Bay campsite, or alternatively the Delta Grove Campground or other campgrounds at Cultus Lake if those are full.

The cost is $18.50 per night per family (2 adults and 2 children, one car), with a possibility of 2 families sharing a site (for a total of up to 8 people per site, including children, and two cars) subject to an additional fee. There is a reservation fee of $6.47 per night. Double sites are also available. More details may be obtained at the BC Government site.

Reservations should be made by each family, three months prior to the camping trip, preferably on the morning of Friday May 17th, starting at 7:30 am to ensure getting a site. If you do not book on that day, the campsite may be full if you try and book later. For online reservation, consult the Discover Camping web site. When making the reservation, identify yourself as an FCC family and ask the attendant, if possible, to assign your family a site close to other FCC families.

Some families will reserve for more than one night. Saturday, August 17th is the day and night planned for FCC families to meet.

Cultus Lake located nearby, offers many services and recreational options. There are hot showers and flush toilets! David Robinson best highlights the campground in the following paragraph:

"There are several camp grounds some by the lake and some in wooded areas. The sites vary in size. Some sites offer space for 2 or more families. Booking is usually 3 months in advance."

There are shops in the vicinity so those who like their coffee and newspaper in the morning can rest easy. There are also a variety of restaurants including quite a good Chinese one. Those that like McDonald's for breakfast only have to go a few kilometres for this! Lots of activities for young children to blow off steam: playgrounds, mini golf, crazy golf, water slides (more for kids 7 and up), go carts, horse riding and of course swimming and boating in the lake. If it is raining, there is Chilliwack and Abbotsford nearby with facilities to offer. Abbotsford has a fun wave pool.

The beauty of Cultus Lake is the fact it is relatively near and has something to offer everyone. It's a lovely area and offers some nice walks. If people do not want to camp, there are nearby motels and the like. Alternatively people can come up for the day. There is a central notice board, so we can post notices of any group activities.

Once we know how many campers will join our group, we can plan together a pot luck or barbecue for the Saturday night.

Please mark it on your calendar, and let me know by email if you are planning on joining the FCC Camping Weekend.

Warmest regards to everyone.
