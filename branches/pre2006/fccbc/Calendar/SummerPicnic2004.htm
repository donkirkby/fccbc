FCC Picnic time again!!!

We are thrilled to be able to tell you about the upcoming, and very exciting, annual FCC Family Picnic. As always the picnic will be held at Ryall Park in Queensborough, New Westminster from 11 am to 3 pm on Sunday, July 4. Ryall Park is located at the Queensborough Community Centre at 920 Ewen Ave New Westminster, B.C. Phone: 604-525-7388. Directions below.

This year we have a wonderful entertainer for children and adults alike who will do face painting, a magic show and more. There will also be the exciting addition of a soccer game for those 6 and older - and you guessed it - parents on one team - kids on the other! Should be loads of fun. The entertainer will perform between 11:15 and 1:15 with the soccer game getting under way at 1:30 pm. The soccer game is for children 6 and older

The cost will be $7.00 for member families and $12.00 for non-member families. We would encourage all families, including those waiting to travel to China, to come and participate. It's a wonderful way to meet new friends and renew acquaintainces. We will have name tags this year and will identify in a special way those families who are waiting to travel. If you see one of these tags, please take a moment to make these new families feel particularly welcome.

Also, the "book program" initiated by Julia Olsen was such a success last year we are repeating it. Please encourage your children to choose books they want to donate and they will be sold for $1.00 each with all proceeds going to orphanages in China for the children still in care. This is another wonderful way to show our children that we continue to support their birth country and the orphanages.

Please remember to bring your own lunch, although this year we will have some special "treats" donated by a local community store and bakery. The excitement continues!

No advance registration needed. Hope to see you there.

Directions to Ryall Park:

From Surrey / Delta Cross the Pattullo Bridge and take the Royal Ave. exit off bridge. Turn right on Stewartson Way. Continue onto the Queensborough Bridge and turn left at first light (Howes St.) then left at stop sign (Ewen Ave.). Turn right into parking lot.

From Richmond Follow Hwy 91 towards the Queensborough Bridge and turn right at the light before the bridge (Howes St.). Turn left at stop sign (Ewen Ave.) and right into parking lot.

From Burnaby / Columbia Traveling south on North Rd. which becomes East Columbia, turn right on Braid St. which becomes 8th Ave. Turn left on 20th St. At bottom of hill turn right, then merge left onto the Queensborough Bridge. Turn left at the first light (Howes St.) and left at the stop sign (Ewen Ave.). Turn right into the parking lot.

Here is a map of New Westminster http://www.nwpr.bc.ca/parks%20web%20page/pictures/facility%20map21.html - Ryall Park is at #8 on the map. Parking is available on Salter Street, which is closer to the Ryall Park Picnic shelter, as well as the Queensborough Community Centre parking lot off Ewen Ave
