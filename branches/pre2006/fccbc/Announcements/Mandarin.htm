MANDARIN INSTRUCTION - New Option for Vancouver Area Children

Xhiaoyan Yue (Lucy), a Mandarin teacher with impressive credentials and recommendations, recently moved to Vancouver from Portland, OR. She will soon be starting Mandarin classes for children in South Vancouver on Saturday mornings. Fees are reasonable, $100 for a single child for the 10 week program, or $150 for a family with more than one child. Ms. Yue has two masters degrees and has taught English and Chinese in China and the United States. For details, contact Ms. Yue directly at tel: 604-325-8356 or at email: xlyue@juno.com.
