Passage to the Heart Update

Amy Klatzkin

I'm delighted to report the publisher's latest good news. In just six months, A Passage to the Heart has met its production costs, and Brian Boyd, the publisher, is preparing to donate $5,000, half each to Amity and the Foundation for Chinese Orphanages. This brings the book's year-to-date charitable contributions up to $7,000!

These donations wouldn't be possible without your assistance with the book and your continuing support. Please pass on our heartfelt thanks to the authors in your chapter. It's because of their good work that people continue to buy the book and support its charitable objectives.
