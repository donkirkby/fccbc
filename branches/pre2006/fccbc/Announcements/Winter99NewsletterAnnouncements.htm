Winter 1999 Newsletter Announcements

New Positions

Our new Treasurer, Cathy McDormand was appointed at the recently held Annual General Meeting. Also, Robin Draper of Prince George and Christine James, also of Prince George, have volunteered to share the Northern BC Representative Position.

Volunteers Needed

Volunteers willing to field questions from prospective parents adopting from China are needed. If you are interested, please contact Wanda Pierson or Marlee Groening

FCC BC & NWWA Membership Renewals

Just a reminder to send in your renewal forms if you haven't already. To date we have received 44 renewals which equates to 33% of the current membership. Half of the membership fee goes to printing and mailing out the newsletter (about $2.00/issue). This will be the last issue you will receive unless you have renewed or renew before March 10, 2000.
