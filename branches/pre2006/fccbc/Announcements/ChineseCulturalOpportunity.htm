Chinese Cultural Opportunity

Nacel Open Door (St. Paul, Minnesota) is currently expecting over 50 high school students from China, as participants in the US government-approved exchange program, from August 25, 1999, through June 14, 2000. Students live with a volunteer host family, bring their own spending money, and are covered by Nacel's organizational insurance policy and support network. The students attend a local high school, and in the past have been very well received.

They would very much like to encourage this opportunity with other families, as a way for them to share with their adopted Chinese children, the native Chinese culture.

Interested parties can contact Nacel Open Door, either by phone (800-622-3553) or email: nacel@nacelopendoor.org, for additional information.
