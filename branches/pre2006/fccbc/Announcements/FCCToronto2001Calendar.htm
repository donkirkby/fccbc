FCC Toronto 2001 Calendar

FCC Toronto has produced a calendar for the year 2001. Similar to last year, this year’s calendar Blessings highlights photographs of children adopted from China. Each month has special interest days along with Chinese, Canadian and American holidays.

The calendar is available and can be ordered for a cost of $20.00 CDN ($15.00 US) payable by cheque to FCC Toronto. Bulk rates for the calendar are available (50 or more to the same address for $17.50 CDN).

Order the calendar from:

Ray Lewis
63 Shady Pine Circle
Brampton, Ontario L6R 1K3

Inquiries can be directed to Ray at (905) 790-9635 or you can e-mail him.

A portion of the proceeds will be donated to Families with Children from China Charitable Fund. This charitable organization is separate from FCC Toronto and is recognized by Canada Customs and Revenue Agency. It’s mandate is to carry out charitable activities in support of orphaned and abandoned children in China. Last year’s calendar sales generated in a $5658 contribution being made. Tax deductible donations can be mailed to:
