FCC Toronto Year 2000 Calendar

FCC Toronto is thrilled to announce that the Year 2000 calendar is almost ready. The professionally produced calendar has full colour photos of nearly 70 of their children having fun, being serious, posing with friends or family - all of them looking absolutely wonderful.

Also included in each month are samples of Chinese writing that can be used to teach our children some simple words related to the various seasons. The calendar is bilingual and annotated with Canadian, Chinese and American holidays. For those people who are just starting the adoption process, we have included contact information for Canadian adoption agencies.

The calendars will be ready by mid-September. For further information contact:
