Adoption Expense Bill

Eric Lowther, Reform MP for Calgary Centre, has brought a private members bill to parliament, seeking to have up to $7,000 of the expenses parents incur undergoing adoption made tax deductible.

Mr. Lowther is asking everyone who supports the bill to fill in a petition, and return it to him, or to your member of parliament. We've created an online version of the petition, that our members can print off and fill out if they would like to add their support.

The bill is available for reading on Parliament's web site. We would encourage everyone to take a look at it first, before submitting the petition. It's short and to the point.
