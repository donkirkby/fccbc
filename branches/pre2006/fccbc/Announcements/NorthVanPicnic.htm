Picnic in North Van

David Robinson sent in the following information:

"Rick, I have organised an ad hoc picnic for all the families on the North Shore who have adopted from China at Lawson Park in Ambleside (at the seafront in the middle of Ambleside near Safeways-17th Ave) for Saturday Sept. 2 at 11.45am.We will meet near the pavilion and toilets (just across from the playground). Note, a lot of the parking in the immediate vicinity is for 2 hours only.

"It's bring your own picnic and games.

"Although it's for North Shore everyone is welcome!"
