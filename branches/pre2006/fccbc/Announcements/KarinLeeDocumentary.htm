Documentary on FCC Families

The following letter was received from Karin Lee highlighting her documentary currently in production. ?Ed.

September 2nd, 1999

To: Association of Families with Children from China

My name is Karin Lee and I am an independent filmmaker from Vancouver working with Shan Tam and her company Holiday Pictures, also an independent producer. We are in the process of making a film about Canadian families with children from China. We began conducting interviews with parents and children all across Canada since May of this year. We have heard many interesting stories from St. Johns', Newfoundland; Montreal, Quebec; Toronto, Ontario; Regina, Saskatchewan, and Vancouver, B.C.

The documentary will show the unique experiences of Canadian families with adopted girls from China, ranging in age from six months to twelve years old. These families will be the nucleus of the film, and will show us how they raise their children, and how their communities and peers support the development of their children's cultural identity as Chinese-Canadians. It will explore the diverse environments of these adopted girls and the ways in which they develop a sense of identity, cultural roots, and inter-racial dynamics.

Families from across Canada have generously agreed to allow us into their lives and to visit their homes. We are currently still seeking Chinese-Canadian families who have adopted from China. If you are interested in participating in our research and documentary, please call Karin Lee at (604) 873-0708 or email me at: leesistr@unixg.ubc.ca.

This one hour documentary will be shown on CTV in August 2,000. We would like to continue filming this fall and next spring. We are particularly interested in following a family and their child(ren) back to China for a visit. If you or your family are planning to visit China next spring, please contact us and we can discuss this trip with you further.

I hope that we can work together to make this important documentary a reality. Thank you for your help and consideration.

Sincerely,

Karin Lee, Writer/Director

Shan Tam, Producer - Holiday Pictures
