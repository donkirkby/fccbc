ANNUAL GENERAL MEETING - 2004

Please join us for our upcoming annual general meeting on Saturday November 13. This is a time for us to interact, collectively reflect on/provide input into our priorities and activities, and have some fun. There will be supervision of and activities for the children (little and bigger ones), a photo slide show, and a wonderful guest presenter. Many of you have seen Dr. Jan Walls “in action” at our Chinese New Years celebrations a few years ago. For those who haven’t seen him before, you are in for a treat!

We’ve shifted venues to Queensborough Community Centre, which many of us are familiar with from the summer picnic. It provides free parking, child-friendly spaces, staff trained in leading children’s programs and an adjacent park if it’s nice weather.

We hope to see you there. Please rsvp (number of people and ages of children coming) to me by November 5.

Kathy 


FCC BC Chinese Culture Event and Annual General Meeting

Saturday November 13, 2004

2:00 to 4:00 pm



Queensborough Community Centre

920 Ewen Avenue, New Westminster - Phone: 604-525-7388

Please note our new venue this year! - It’s adjacent to the Ryall Picnic Shelter, where we have our summer picnic.

Featuring GUEST PRESENTATION by Dr. Jan Walls Director of the Asia-Canada Program, at Simon Fraser University on Chinese wit and humour Dr. Walls will regale us with his own unique style of Chinese rapping

Annual General Meeting will include:

  • Updates on key FCC BC activities
  • Overview of how we are doing with our 10 year plan
  • And a slide presentation from FCC BC in action! (please forward any photos, which we will gladly include!)

    There will be lots of activities for the children:

  • younger children ages 1-5 will be supervised by trained leaders
  • children ages 6 and over will join for the presentation by Dr. Jan Walls, then have interactive activities with community centre leaders. We might have access to the gymnasium – to be confirmed closer to the date.

    There is no registration cost. However, please rsvp by November 5th to kcopeman@telus.net so that we can ensure sufficient seating for the event, and supervision for the children.

    Directions to the Queensborough Community Centre

    From Surrey / Delta Cross the Pattullo Bridge and take the Royal Ave. exit off bridge. Turn right on Stewartson Way. Continue onto the Queensborough Bridge and turn left at first light (Howes St.) then left at stop sign (Ewen Ave.). Turn right into parking lot.

    From Richmond Follow Hwy 91 towards the Queensborough Bridge and turn right at the light before the bridge (Howes St.). Turn left at stop sign (Ewen Ave.) and right into parking lot.

    From Burnaby / Columbia Traveling south on North Rd. which becomes East Columbia, turn right on Braid St. which becomes 8th Ave. Turn left on 20th St. At bottom of hill turn right, then merge left onto the Queensborough Bridge. Turn left at the first light (Howes St.) and left at the stop sign (Ewen Ave.). Turn right into the parking lot.
