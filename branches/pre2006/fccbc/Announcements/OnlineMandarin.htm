Online Mandarin Course Available

Want to learn Mandarin online? Check out the website of the World Wide Language Institute. Courses start once a month, and go for ten weeks. Cost is approximately $59.00US per course. We have no idea how good the courses are, or who the company is, but it's a good option for people who need some flexibility in their studies.
