Raising Healthy Multiracial Adoptive Families Booklet

The Adoptive Parents Association of B.C. is proud (and relieved) to announce that after a year and a half of collaboration with adoptive parents, counsellors, and diversity consultants, that it has completed its 72-page booklet Raising Healthy Multiracial Adoptive Families. The book is set up in an easy-to-read question-and-answer format and addresses such topics as Sharing Your Story, Culture and Heritage, Speaking Up, School Issues, Stereotypes, and Racism. Also included is a detailed glossary and bibliography.

It is available for $12 including shipping and handling. Reduced bulk order rates are available upon request. Contact the APABC at apabcinfo@bcadoption.com or call (604) 588-7300.
