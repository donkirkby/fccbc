PETITION (Tax Deduction for Adoption Expenses)

TO THE HONOURABLE HOUSE OF COMMONS OF CANADA IN PARLIAMENT ASSEMBLED

WE THE UNDERSIGNED CITIZENS OF CANADA, draw the attention of the House to the following:

WHEREAS, adoptive parents make a significant social contribution to our society;

AND WHEREAS adoptive parents often face significant adoption-related costs, but out-of-pocket adoption expenses are not tax deductible;

THEREFORE your petitioners pray that Parliament pass Bill C-505, introduced by the Member of Parliament for Calgary Centre, in order to provide a deduction of up to $7,000 for expenses related to the adoption of a child.
