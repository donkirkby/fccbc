Chinese Adoption Mailing Lists

There are a number of mailing lists regarding Chinese adoption. These lists allow you to participate with a community of people with the same interests, by sending them e-mail, and receiving e-mail from them. Some of these lists, such as the post and pre China adoption lists, tend to be populated by very opinionated people, who often take great joy in cutting any opinion to quivering shreds. However, others are harmless, and fun, and sources of excellent information.

I've given up keeping track of lists. These are sites that are doing a better job of it.
