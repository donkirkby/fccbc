  • 2005.10.14 AGM

    AGM details:
    Saturday November 12, 2005
    10 am - 12 noon, followed by lunch which will be ordered in Queensborough Community Centre.

    Our AGM is an important time to look back on our accomplishments, acknowledge the contributions of our many volunteers and confirm the key priorities for the coming year(s)

    Child care will be provided for the younger children (up to age 5) in an adjacent preschool room There will be activities for ages 6 and up, including learning and then performing a Chinese dance - to be taught by Lorita Leung Dance Academy

    No registration fee - Donation welcomed for FCC Charitable Fund. For those planning to attend, please rsvp by November 1, to kcopeman@telus.net

  • 2005.03.13 Jane Brown

    Jane Brown will be coming to Kelowna, BC on July 22-23, 2005. Please ensure early registration. Full details can be seen at http://www.adoption-bc.com/index.html. To register, please print out the brochure and registration form (2MB Word Document) and send it to the Adoption Centre or call 250-763-8002 or 1-800-935-4237.

    I checked out suitable family motel type places to stay at in Kelowna and these are 3 that seem ok. I have stayed at the Sandlewood Inn a few years back and it was great as it was opposite Gyro beach with its wonderful playground on the beach. All 3 motels are near shops and eating places and the centre of town is readily accessible. So those visiting Kelowna for the Playshops may like to consider the above. Anyone going to the Playshops MUST book a place to stay well in advance as they get fully booked very quickly.
