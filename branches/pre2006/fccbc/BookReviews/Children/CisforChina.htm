C is for China

by Sungwan So, Published in 1997 by Frances Lincoln Limited, London, England

© by Heather McDonald - Vancouver BC

This book is an "ABC" book aimed at children from four through seven years of age. Using photographs from Guangdong, Guangxi, Yunnan and Shanxi provinces, the author illustrates the common beliefs held by Chinese people and some of their customs in an "ABC" format.

The book is a geographical, historical and cultural resource that will endure through the primary years of school. From "Abacus" to "Lantern", from "Jade" to "Wenzi", this photographic alphabet presents Chinese culture in such an appealing way that in fact a person of any age will be captivated by it.
