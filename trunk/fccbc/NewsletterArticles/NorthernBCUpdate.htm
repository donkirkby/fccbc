Northern BC Update

By Christine James, FCCBC & NWWA

Northern BC chapter of FCC is primarily based in Prince George, but welcomes members from throughout the northern half of the province. We meet on an informal basis, and enjoy seeing the growth and development in our families and children. There are currently six families in Prince George with children from China, two families who are "waiting", and one family in the application stage. Our children range in age from two to nine years.

The FCC group greatly enjoyed participating in the AFABC Christmas party and sleigh ride held on Sunday Dec 16th at Noah's Ark in Prince George. An informal potluck and outdoor games day was held on Tuesday January 1st, with five families and associated friends in attendance. The next event is tentatively scheduled for Friday February 15th in celebration of Chinese New Year's. This dinner and entertainment will be held at the China Sail restaurant in Prince George.

One of our school age children has been attending Chinese lessons (through a local Chinese organization) since last September. Chinese language lessons for preschool children will begin Monday January 14th in the evenings.
