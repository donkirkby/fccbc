This site is in China , maintained by the Chinese government departments responsible for managing adoptions in China . In both English and Chinese; to see the Chinese characters, you'll need a chinese character set loaded into your browser.

Our Chinese Daughters Foundation http://www.ocdf.org/ocdf/ supports adoptive families in many ways including China tours, resources and fundraising for children in orphanages.

Association for Research in International Adoption http://www.adoption-research.org/ disseminates research findings to the international adoption community. The site contains links for adoptive parents to learn more about the issues that may impact internationally adopted children
