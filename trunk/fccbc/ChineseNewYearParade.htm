New Year's Parade - Vancouver

This is an event worth taking in, year after year. It starts on Pender Street in Chinatown at 11:00 am. There are lots of people from the community, Lion and Dragon dancers, a few floats, and lots of firecrackers. The Lion dancers then go from business to business, dancing in front of the doors; the owners come out and give them money, to ensure good luck for the coming year.

This year the parade is on January 28th.
