Our mission

  • To provide a peer group for parents and children
  • To schedule events reflecting the culture of our children's birthplace
  • To provide information on adoption to our members and others interested in adopting from China
  • To help educate on the experience and issues of adopting children from China
  • To support fundraising to benefit children in orphanages in China
  • To celebrate our families!

AFABC Cross-Cultural Adoption

from the events page

  • Jan 19, Mar 8, April 19, May 31
  • Time: 9am to 3pm
  • AFABC Office, 200 - 7342 Winston Street, Burnaby. (Need directions? See Google maps.)
  • Cost: Per person: $75 AFABC Members / $85 Non-members.

The Adoptive Families Association of BC invites you to attend this workshop if you are planning to adopt a child of different race or culture, locally or internationally, with an Agency or MCFD. Come to AFABC, network with other families, learn about Attachment concerns for children and their families, potential Medical Issues and family matters related to Race, Culture and Heritage. Also, meet a panel of Cross-Cultural Adoptive Parents and Adoptees.

Phone AFABC at 604-320-7330, e-mail , or visit www.bcadoption.com.
