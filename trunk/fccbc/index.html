Our mission

  • To provide a peer group for parents and children
  • To schedule events reflecting the culture of our children's birthplace
  • To provide information on adoption to our members and others interested in adopting from China
  • To help educate on the experience and issues of adopting children from China
  • To support fundraising to benefit children in orphanages in China
  • To celebrate our families!

Lotus Gala, October 17 2006

from the events page

A special evening in aid of Half the Sky Foundation

  • Tuesday October 17, 2006 6:15pm - 10:00pm
  • Pink Pearl Restaurant 1132 E. Hastings St., Vancouver
  • Silent auction - fabulous prizes
  • Entertainment by the Orchid Ensemble
  • Presentation by Yves Maurais, founder of HTS Canada
  • 9-course Chinese dinner

Come join us for an evening of celebration and appreciation of the work of Half the Sky Foundation, a charity dedicated to providing education and enrichment programs for children in orphanages in China. Help make a difference in their lives.

A fun night out for parents with a 9-course Chinese dinner and silent auction. Lots of fabulous items to bid on! Live music entertainment and a presentation by Yves Maurais, founder of Half the Sky Foundation-Canada. Master of ceremonies: Fred Lee, CBC Radio's man about town.

Tickets are $45 each. Call the Adoptive Families Association of BC at (604)320-7730 or e-mail .

The Lotus Gala is also supported by AFABC, Family Services of Greater Vancouver, Sunrise Family Services and The Adoption Centre.
