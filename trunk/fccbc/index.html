Our mission

  • To provide a peer group for parents and children
  • To schedule events reflecting the culture of our children's birthplace
  • To provide information on adoption to our members and others interested in adopting from China
  • To help educate on the experience and issues of adopting children from China
  • To support fundraising to benefit children in orphanages in China
  • To celebrate our families!

Join the Adoptive Families Association of BC in the Christmas festivities and in finding homes for kids in need! AFABC celebrated Christmas early in 2005, wrapping up the IKEA Christmas Tree Sale fundraising event four days early due to a tree sell out. We raised $5,500 for the Family for Every Child program by selling 4,000 trees! Come out this season and help make a difference in kids' lives.
