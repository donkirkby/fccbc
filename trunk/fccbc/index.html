Our mission

  • To provide a peer group for parents and children
  • To schedule events reflecting the culture of our children's birthplace
  • To provide information on adoption to our members and others interested in adopting from China
  • To help educate on the experience and issues of adopting children from China
  • To support fundraising to benefit children in orphanages in China
  • To celebrate our families!

Annual FCC Family Day & Report to Members - Sat 8 Nov 2008

from the events page

  • 10 am - 1 pm
  • Vancity International Film Theatre
  • 1181 Seymour St. (at Davie), Vancouver

Join us at a new venue - the luxurious Vancity Theatre. Enjoy a China-related film in state-of-the-art theatre conditions and have a say in what's happening to FCC BC. Experience spectacular imagery of China on the big screen and take a walk through China's oldest and some say most spectacular village, the Ancient City of Phoenix. These films are appropriate for all ages; we'll even have a balcony area reserved for kids deemed old enough by their parents. Not forgetting the wee ones, they'll have the use of an open play area. To help cover costs, there'll be a nominal charge, say $4 per adult; children free. Please pre-register (e-mail ) as space is limited due to theatre seating.
