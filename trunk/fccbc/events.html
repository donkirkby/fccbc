Our Annual Summer Picnic Sun Jul 6, 2008 (Not Canada Day)

  • 10 am - 3 pm
  • Ryall Park, 920 Ewen Ave (Need directions? See Google maps.)
  • Volunteers needed!

It's our ever-popular FCC BC Summer Picnic at Ryall Park, next to the Queensborough Community Centre, New Westminister. Picnic starts at 10 am. Don't forget sun screen, towels & bathing suits for those wishing to use the water-park. Only $10 per family, members. ($20 per family, non-members.) We need volunteers to help run the picnic. Please contact Danielle Soulodre () or Eamon Duffy (). This year, we will also enlist the help of our older daughters 11+ to prepare hotdogs and snacks. Any monies raised from food sales will be donated to a charity of the girls' choosing. The Tim Horton community cruiser will be on hand from 11 am - 1 pm.
