Events

Chinese New Year Celebration, Saturday February 10th, 2007

  • 11am - 3pm at Floata Restaurant
  • 180 Keefer St., Vancouver, B.C. (Need directions? See Google maps.)
  • free parking under the restaurant
  • Send in the registration form

Chinese New Year 2007 will bring with it lots of changes - but never fear - the best parts remain! On Feb 10, 2007, we will be having our annual Chinese New Year party at the fabulous Floata restaurant in Chinatown. The party will be held from 11am to 3pm with some great crafts to start off the day for the children. This new year will see us having a sit-down lunch with some interesting new items on the menu!

The restaurant is a beautiful big venue in the heart of Chinatown with great acoustics and we will have some wonderful entertainment for you to enjoy. We are also happy to have parking provided this year. Simply bring your parking ticket with you to the restaurant. The registration form is on the back page. Please note the address for mailing as well as some changes to the registration. If you are inviting extended family or friends to the event, please remember they are eligible for the non-member price.
