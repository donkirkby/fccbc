FCC BC Mailing List

We have created a mailing list, using Yahoo! Groups, for the use of FCC BC and its members. This list is for sending information out to all FCC BC families, sharing information about events, people, media information, in short, just about anything that FCC families want to discuss. It will replace our old static mailing list.

It gives all members of the executive the ability to send information out to the list, and gives members the chance to discuss issues. What this list is not for is for gossip, anger, or as a place to sell commercial items, (unless they are directly useful to FCC BC members).

The list is moderated - all notes are first approved by the webmaster before they are passed on. Inappropriate notes will not be sent to the list.

  • To be added to the list, send a note to fcc_bc@yahoo.ca saying who you are, and why you want to joing.

  • To be deleted from the list, send a note to fcc_bc@yahoo.ca, asking to be deleted from the list.

  • To send mail to the list, send a note to fccbcgroup@yahoogroups.ca
