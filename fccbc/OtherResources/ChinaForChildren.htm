China for Children

Our Chinese Daughters Foundation, Inc., a non-profit organization supporting families who have adopted children from China have developed the children’s magazine China for Children. This quarterly publication is full of activities, facts, songs, and cultural information . Subscriptions can be obtained by sending a cheque for $32.00 US made out to OCDF, and sent to:
