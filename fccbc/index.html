Our mission

  • To provide a peer group for parents and children
  • To schedule events reflecting the culture of our children's birthplace
  • To provide information on adoption to our members and others interested in adopting from China
  • To help educate on the experience and issues of adopting children from China
  • To support fundraising to benefit children in orphanages in China
  • To celebrate our families!

Summer Picnic July 1, 2007

from the events page

You are invited to our Annual FCCBC Summer Picnic at the Queensborough Community Centre in New Westminster Sunday, July 1st from 11-3. We will be at the Ryall 1 and Zazzara ball fields. (Need directions? See Google maps.)

This is an opportunity to network with old and new friends and celebrate Canada Day in the spirit of family and fun!

Cost for FCCBC families is $10.00 and cost for non-members is $20.00/family. Payment can be made on arrival.

This year there will be plenty of fun and games for kids and adults of all ages and we will be providing hotdogs, smokies and usual picnic goodies.

Don't forget to bring your sun screen, towels and bathing suits!!!

MARK YOUR CALENDARS NOW! WE LOOK FORWARD TO SEEING YOU THERE!
