Our mission

  • To provide a peer group for parents and children
  • To schedule events reflecting the culture of our children's birthplace
  • To provide information on adoption to our members and others interested in adopting from China
  • To help educate on the experience and issues of adopting children from China
  • To support fundraising to benefit children in orphanages in China
  • To celebrate our families!

An Evening with Jane Liedtke November 2, 2007

from the events page

  • China, Coffee and Connecting...
  • Friday 6-10 pm
  • $10 per person (for room and refreshments).
  • We request that you to bring a favourite appetizer or dessert to share.
  • RSVP to Eamon at

Come and join other FCC parents for a relaxing evening in the beautiful setting of the Shadbolt Centre. Following conversation over appetizers and dessert, Jane Liedtke, Founder/CEO of Our Chinese Daughters Foundation will share from her in-depth knowledge of traveling back to China (what are things to think about in planning a trip? what would the experience be like for our children?). Parents (e.g. Debbie Matheson, Peter Gibson and Marlee Groening) who have returned to China will be available to answer your questions and share their families' experiences.

This is a rare opportunity to meet Jane, who having lived extensively in China is a fabulous resource. Check out her foundation website (above) to get a sense of what she has accomplished and what she will able to share with us.

Even if you aren't planning to return to China in the near future, come and join us. Invite your travel group parents or other friends. This is a rare opportunity for parents to connect with one another 'sans kids'! Please feel free to forward this e-mail to friends who have adopted that you feel might be interested. We look forward to seeing you there!

Many thanks to Kathy Copeman-Stewart for arranging this coup!
