FCC BC is a network for families in British Columbia that have adopted children from China . Our mission is to:

  • Provide a peer group for parents and children
  • Schedule events reflecting the culture of our children's birthplace
  • Provide information on adoption to our members and others interested in adopting in China
  • Help to educate on the experience and issues of adopting children from China
  • Support fundraising to benefit children in orphanages in China
  • Celebrate our families!
