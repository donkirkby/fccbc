News Groups

There are a number of news groups on China on the internet. News groups come and go, so I'm not going to list them, but by starting up your news reader and doing a search for 'China' or 'Chinese' you'll find them. If you've never used the news groups, (also called Usenet) before, be aware of the 'dangers'. First, they tend to be populated by very opinionated people, who often take great joy in cutting any opinion to quivering shreds. The other problem is that spammers troll the news groups to pick up live e-mail accounts; if you post to a news group, you're likely to start getting all kinds of interesting junk e-mail.
