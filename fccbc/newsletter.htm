FCC BC Newsletter - Friends of the Family

Friends of the family is presently published twice a year. The intention is to make this at least four times a year, in order to keep our members up to date regarding events and issues that affect them.

The newsletter is dedicated to providing information about what is going on in our community. It also discusses what it means to be a multi-cultural adoptive family, and helps address the difficulties in bringing Chinese culture into our children's lives. We also bring news on what is going on in the adoption community, adoption laws, and in China.

We actively encourage the participation of members and non-members in supplying material. We are looking for articles, information pieces, recipes, book reviews, movie reviews: in short, anything you think might be of interest to the members of the FCC.
