New Year's Parade - Vancouver

This is an event worth taking in, year after year. It starts on Pender Street in Chinatown between 11 and noon. There are lots of people from the community, Lion and Dragon dancers, a few floats, and lots of firecrackers. The Lion dancers then go from business to business, dancing in front of the doors; the owners come out and give them money, to ensure good luck for the coming year.

For more information about the historical and cultural meanings of the festival, see our New Year's Links.

This year the parade is on February 17th.
