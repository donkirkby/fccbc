Chinese Cultural Centre of Greater Vancouver

Chinese Cultural Camp

Objective

The Cultural Camp is designed for children ages 5-10 raised in an English speaking environment. Through different activities such as field trips and crafts children can increase their interest in knowing about the Chinese Culture.

Activities

Learning Mandarin, calligraphy, crafts, Chinese music and dance, Chinese traditional food making, Chinese painting and field trips.
