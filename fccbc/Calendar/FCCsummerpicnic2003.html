FCC 2003 Summer Picnic

Just to advise preliminary details on the FCC 2003 summer picnic. The date will be Saturday, July 5, 2003. Location is at the Ryall Park picnic shelter, next to Queensborough community centre, 920 Ewan Street, New Westminster, B.C. Time will be from 11 am until 3 pm. More details later in the Spring regarding entertainment etc....
