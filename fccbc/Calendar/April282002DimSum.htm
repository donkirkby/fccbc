DIM SUM April 28th

Dimsum has been arranged at the Garden Villa Seafood Restaurant at 127 E. Pender Street in Chinatown for 11 am on Sunday April 28. We will be in the back area which allows for some privacy but more importantly for little ones to roam a bit.

Please telephone the Manager at 604-688-3877 in advance if you are coming and provide her with your family name and number coming. Inform her that you are part of the FCC party arranged by David Robinson.

I think they will set up tables for 10 as I have indicated I expect 30 to 40 people, so please just grab a seat and let others sit at your table. Dimsum is brought round on carts or can be ordered seperately. If anyone wants noodle dishes etc again just order what you like. The dimsum here is excellent. It's up to each table how you order and how you arrange payment of the bill. You can either share everything or select dishes for you own family.

The restaurant is just up from the Chinese Cultural Centre but on the opposite side of the road. You have to climb one set of stairs to get to
the restaurant.

There are some lovely Chinese shops nearby for kids and adults to browse after dimsum.

One road behind CCC there is a carpark at $1 per hour.

David Robinson
