FCCBC AGM 2002

1:30 to 3:30 pm
Century House, Moody Park, 620 8th Street, New Westminster

We have come a long way this year in terms of improving communications, Newsletter and events, thanks to a dedicated Executive and volunteers. There is lots more happening behind the scenes and the AGM is the time to catch up with what is happening, how you wish to shape the future of FCC as well as catch up with old friends.

I encourage everyone to come to the AGM including children. The children will be able to do some arts and crafts. The Executive will give a short outline of what they are doing and there will be some discussion about the Website and Jane Brown's Playshops to be held in mid March of next year. Then it is open invitation to you to ask questions and give comments/views.

I ask that people bring snacks/cakes/fruit or biscuits to be shared. We hope to have tea and coffee available.

I look forward to seeing lots of you there.

If anyone has a specific topic that they wish to raise at the AGM, would they please let me know in advance, if possible.
