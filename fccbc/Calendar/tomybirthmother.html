The National Film Board Club presents a free screening of it's documentary, "To My Birthmother", to be followed by a stimulating post-film discussion on Tuesday, April 29, 7PM at the Pacific Cinematheque (1131 Howe Street, Vancouver).

The discussion will be led by Judy Robertson of Cineworks, who will be joined by panellists Joan Vanstone of Parent Finders and Nancy Kato of the Forget Me Not Family Society.

Delving into the past can be risky business, especially for someone who's adopted. When Mary Klaasen went looking for her birthmother, she discovered that trying to locate her would take perseverance and guts, and that succeeding in the search was not the end, but another beginning. This video diary recounts Mary's personal journey to find the woman who gave her life. It's a fascinating story of a reunion fraught with suspense, humour and vitality. This poignant film is directed by Beverly Shaffer, and produced by Shaffer and Mark Zannis.
