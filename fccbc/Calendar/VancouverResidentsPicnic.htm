Vancouver Residents’ Picnic

By Angela Schiwy

Attention all Vancouver residents!! 

 We will be holding our 3rd annual informal gathering of Vancouver families this summer, on Saturday, July 27th, 5:00 pm.  (Please note last year's attendees - our date has changed to July.)  A couple of years ago we had the idea of gathering together those who lived in Vancouver itself, so that we'd get to know the families living nearby a bit better.  So for the second year in a row, Lyn and Lloyd Baron have offered their Southlands heritage "estate" for the gathering.  We had a wonderful time last year - thanks Lyn & Lloyd!  They have a large trampoline and a rope swing in the yard.  We will be having a potluck on the grass, so bring folding chairs.  And be sure to bring some wine for the dinner. 

For further details please call Angela Schiwy at 604.738.6590, and I will add your family to the existing mailing list.
