FCC BC Summer Picnic

Three legged race? Egg toss? Water park? Mark your calendars for the Annual FCCBC Summer picnic.

  • Sunday July 2, 2006 , 11 am to 3 pm
  • Picnic Shelter at Ryall Park Spraypark 920 Ewen Avenue (behind Queensborough Community Centre) New Westminster (Need directions? See Google maps.)

The cost is $10 per family for members and $20 per family for non-members. No pre-registration is required. All waiting families are welcome and encouraged to come. Watch this space for new information or contact Karen Yow at 604.299.8832.

Thanks to our event sponsors Sunrise Soya Foods and Tim Hortons.
