Mid-Autumn Moon Festival Dinner and Lantern Walk

  • Sunday, September 14th, 2008
  • Dinner 5:00 PM (Sold out!)
  • Lantern Walk about 6:45 PM

Join FCC families, fans and friends for dinner at King's Chinese Cuisine at 4488 Main St. on the northeast corner of Main & 29th Ave. We have several large round tables seating ten to twelve people each. Enjoy a ten course set dinner for $10 per person. If you wish to attend, please mail a cheque payable to FCC BC for $10 per person listing your party's names, children's ages and your contact information to Debbie Matheson, 3519 W. 26th Ave. Vancouver, BC, V6S 1N6. Welcome waiting parents and new members to all tables. BYO booster seats. Some high chairs available. Cheques must arrive by Monday, Sept. 8th. Contact or call 604-731-8901.

Bring your lanterns and flashlights up to the lookout at Queen Elizabeth Park, just west of the clock and Seasons Restaurant. Admire colourful lanterns as we wander along the beautiful paths of the sunken gardens. Meet many Chinese-Canadian families enjoying the full moon, sharing tea, fruit and moon cakes, and comparing lanterns with us.

Waiting Families - Vancouver

The next FCCBC Waiting Families get-together is scheduled for Sunday, September 14th. Since this is the Mid-Autumn Moon Festival, we invite Waiting Families to come to the Dinner at 5:00pm to meet other families who are waiting and families who have already adopted in an informal and friendly atmosphere. Please book your tickets through Debbie (see article above).

If you would like to chat about waiting while waiting for dinner, Sheila, Don, and Anna Kirkby will be at the Saltspring Island Coffee Shop at 4295 Main Street (at 27th) from 4:00pm to 5:00pm.
