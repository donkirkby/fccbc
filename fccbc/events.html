From Russia, With Love - Dec. 18th, 2006

  • Airs Monday, December 18 at 9 p.m. on Knowledge Network

Adoption is usually considered a blessing and an escape from a lonely life. But what if it means leaving behind everyone and everything you ever knew and travelling halfway around the world? From Russia, With Love, airing Monday, December 18 at 9 p.m., follows the emotional journey of two girls who must learn to adjust to a new life and family in Canada.

In a remote Russian village, Olga and Lera lived in an orphanage until two Canadian couples came to adopt them. The adjustment was more difficult for these older girls than it would have been for babies, and they both face struggles that linger beneath the surface.

This heartwarming documentary follows the newly made families for three years. However, what begins as an exploration of cross-cultural adoption blooms into a story of human compassion, generosity, and the power of love.

Chinese New Year Celebration, Saturday February 10th, 2007

  • 11am - 3pm at Floata Restaurant

Chinese New Year 2007 will bring with it lots of changes - but never fear - the best parts remain! On Feb 10, 2007, we will be having our annual Chinese New Year party at the fabulous Floata restaurant in Chinatown. The party will be held from 11am to 3pm with some great crafts to start off the day for the children. This new year will see us having a sit-down lunch with some interesting new items on the menu!
