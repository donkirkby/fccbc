New Email Group - China's "Waiting Child" Program

I want to let everyone know that there is a new yahoo group for Canadians that are in the process of adopting a waiting child from China or for those interested in learning about the process from a Canadian perspective. The waiting child program is quite new in Canada, there are only a handful of families in the process so far. You can join at http://groups.yahoo.com/group/cndswaitingchildrenchina

If you have any trouble subscribing, please let me know! Thanks... Kim.
