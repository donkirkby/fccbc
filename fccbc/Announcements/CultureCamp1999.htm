REGISTER FOR THE 1999 HERITAGE CAMP OVERNIGHT

CAMP CASEY, WHIDBEY ISLAND
JULY 9, 10, 11 (Friday Evening To Sunday Afternoon)

Bring your entire family for a weekend of activities with fellow FCC families -- relaxing by the pool, splashing in the new wading pool perfect for our children, delighting in the treasures found on a beach walk, kite-flying, learning Chinese songs, art activities, marching in a children's parade, dining together -- all on sunny Whidbey Island. Visit our website for additional information about the Heritage Camp:

http://ourworld.compuserve.com/homepages/kenkisch/chc.htm

Dormitory style buildings have been reserved with food service provided including a salmon barbecue Saturday evening. Each family will have a private room with twin beds -- with room for a port-a-crib -- and shared restrooms. (For those who are familiar with Camp Casey and our previous year's arrangements -- some accommodations will be in different buildings.)

Cost for accommodations on the Camp Casey campus, including 5 meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner Saturday and breakfast, lunch Sunday) are as follows:

COST PER PERSON FOR 2 NIGHTS LODGING AND 5 MEALS:

ADULT $84
CHILD 4 - 12 years $68
CHILD 0 - 3 years FREE
3rd CHILD (or more) 4 - 12 years $25

Alternative accommodations include first class hotels in nearby Coupeville. Camping is also available nearby on a first-come first-serve basis. Cost of meals and a day-use fee would be charged at Camp Casey. Call Ken Kisch at 425-836-3327 for details.

Ken Kisch

WHAT TO DO NEXT? Full payment is greatly appreciated but we are more than happy to accommodate a payment plan. 1/2 of the final payment will be due April 30 and the other half by May 29. If you have to cancel, we will be able to refund all but the activity fee up until May 29. Because of commitments we have to make to Camp Casey, no refunds can be given after May 29.
