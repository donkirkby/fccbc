Pre-school Activity Class 

Pre-school Activity Class (this class is put on by the Cultural Centre
for all children) 

Mornings from 10:00 to !2:30 especially for 4-6 year olds.


$80.00 per week or $250.00 for the month.


Activities include games, songs, dancing, handicrafts, storey-telling and
some reading. Activities are conducted primarily in Cantonese.


Call 604-658-8870 to register (Ask for Winnie Wong)
