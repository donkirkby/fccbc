UPDATE FROM THE MILLS - April 6, 2000

KIMMY’S WISH CAME TRUE!!

Our family recently returned from a one week trip to Disney World in Florida, courtesy of Make-A-Wish Foundation and Give Kids the World.

It was WONDERFUL!! Everyone was so friendly and accommodating. It was such a safe place and we had a tremendous amount of fun. Kim was in her glory! The only difference amongst all the pictures we took, was the background. Otherwise, they all consisted of a common foreground…a huge smile on Kimmy’s face.

It was sad to have to leave, knowing this fairytale existence would have to come to an end and we would once again, have to face reality. Two days after returning from our trip, Kimberley was back at B.C. Children’s Hospital for another round of Chemotherapy. At the end of this month, the doctors will do a scan to see if this latest Chemo treatment has shrunk the tumour. If not…it’s back to the drawing board, but we’ll be pushing to be included in the imminent clinical trials of that new Cancer Treatment that hit the news recently, referred to as “Metronomic Dosing”. Apparently, scientists in the lab have had tremendous success eradicating tumours in mice by using small, but constant doses of Chemo in combination with drugs that prevent the growth of blood cells, which tumours need in order to grow. Human trials are next and if conventional methods aren’t working on Kimmy, we want on board.

Recently, a B.C. organization called the Coast Foundation Society awarded Kimberley with a certificate, honoring her courage. Admittedly, it stemmed from a letter I submitted as part of the organization’s Courage To Come Back contest, but I felt it was so deserving. Kim is such a brave girl. So inspiring!

And, now that we’ve returned to reality, we’re looking to have our #1 Wish Come True!!

David O. Mills
