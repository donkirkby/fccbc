Transracial Adult Adoptees Panel

Friday, November 19, 1999

A panel of adults of African and Asian heritage adopted transracially into Caucasian families talk about growing up adopted in a predominantly white society.

Century House, Moody Park, New Westminster, 620 8th. Street

(Call 526-2733 for directions)

$5 per person at the door, but please preregister by calling the ACASG Info-line at 469-5594.

From 7 to 9:30 pm.

~Presented by the Afro-Canadian Adoption Support Group and Families With Children From China~
