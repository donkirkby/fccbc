Chinese Culture and Language Day Camp

“A FUN unique enrichment program for Canadian children aged 4 to 14”

This summer program, sponsored by the Vancouver Chinese Culture and Language School (now in it’s 14th year), provides a series of fun activities for children of any background to learn and enjoy Chinese language, art, music, dance and sports. There is a variety of enrollment options available including whole days, half days or language only.

The programs are divided into Preschool and Kindergarten (4 to 5 years old) and Elementary and Secondary programs (age 6 and up). The Mandarin Language class requires children to be at least 8 years of age. All materials for the various crafts, are provided and included in the fee.

Fees are as follows:

Discount of $16-22 if registered for less than 5 weeks in August. Cancellation fees apply.

Registration is on June 14, 1999 (7-8:30 PM) or June 17, 1999 (6-7:30 PM) or by calling (604) 876-9518. Registration is on a first come first served basis. The location of the day camp is at Shaughnessy Elementary School, 4250 Marguerite St., Vancouver.
