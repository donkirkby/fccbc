Chinese Heritage Classes for Children (Correction)

Thompson Community Centre in Richmond has started a series of Mandarin and Cantonese programs. The classes currently being offered are a Mandarin Immersion program and a Chinese Zhu-yin program (traditional characters and symbols). Mandarin classes are held Monday, Wednesday and Friday. The Zhu-yin and Cantonese programs are held Saturday. Fall Session registration will be at the end of August or beginning of September. A 5% Family discount is available if more than 1 child is registered. Call Thompson Community Centre at (604) 272-5338.
