Chinese Heritage Classes for Children

Thompson Community Centre in Richmond has started a series of Mandarin and Cantonese programs. The classes currently being offered are a Mandarin Immersion program and a Chinese Zhu-yin program (traditional characters and symbols). Classes are held Saturday mornings from 0930 – 1130h. Spring Session registration begins March 09, 1999. They are for four to twelve year olds. A 5% Family discount is available if more than 1 child is registered. Call Thompson Community Centre at (604) 272-5338 for further information.
