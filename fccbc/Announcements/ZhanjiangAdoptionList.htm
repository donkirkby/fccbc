Zhanjiang Adoption List

Do you have a child adopted from the city of Zhanjiang, in the Guangdong Province of the People's Republic of China? Do or did you ever live in the city of Zhanjiang? Have you ever visited or are you planning to visit the city of Zhanjiang? This list is for anyone who has an interest in learning more about the city of Zhanjiang and sharing your information about its people and its culture.

"Zhanjiang-Families" is a new email list, and part of the Onelist.com family of lists. Onelist.com is an email subscription service with thousands of email lists you can subscribe to. When you subscribe to a list, such as "Zhanjiang-Families", every time another subscribing member directs an email to the list, you will receive that email in your personal email box.

You can join this community by going to the following web page:

http://www.onelist.com/subscribe/Zhanjiang-Families

Or you can join by sending email to the following address:

Zhanjiang-Families-subscribe@onelist.com
