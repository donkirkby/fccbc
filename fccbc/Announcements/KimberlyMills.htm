Trust Fund for Kimberley Mills

A young girl, Kimberley Mills, adopted into BC from China, has become very ill with cancer. She has undergone treatment at Children's hospital, and is shuttling back and forth between there and her home in Kelowna.

The family is undergoing financial duress, trying to keep up with travel, and their inability to work while in Vancouver for treatments. In addition, Kimberley has said that she would very much like a trip to Disneyland, if her illness allows it.

A trust fund has been set up to collect donations to help out with these and any other expenses incurred by the family as part of this difficult time. If you would like to contribute, send cheques to:

Kimberley Mills In Trust
Account #8266710
c/o Interior Savings Credit Union Mission Branch
595 KLO Road
Kelowna BC V1Y 8E7

We also hope to be taking contributions at the Chinese New Year party.
