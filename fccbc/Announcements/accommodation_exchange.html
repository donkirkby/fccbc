FCC Accommodation Exchange

Many of us enjoy travelling with our children to new destinations. Unfortunately, we live in troubled times and many parts of the world are not as safe as they used to be.

The FCC British Columbia shington chapter, with the assistance of Steve Whan of Autumn Jade Publishing, has set up a free accommodation exchange for FCC families travelling in BC and also those considering either the US or Canada for their next vacation or family trip.

Visit http://www.autumnjade.com/fcc/ where you can register your property for exchange or rental and view what other FCC families have to offer. It's free to register and free to use. Plan a safe vacation knowing that we all share a common bond (our marvelous children).
