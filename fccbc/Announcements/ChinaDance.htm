China in Dance 2000 - Descendants of the Dragon

The Chinese Cultural Centre of Greater Vancouver (CCC) presents an exciting Chinese dance-drama performed by the Asian Performing Arts Theatre of Seattle on Febraury 05, 2000 (Chinese New Year's Day) at the Queen Elizabeth Theatre at 8:00 PM.

Some 50 musicians and dancers from North America and China will perform Chinese music, songs, acrobatics, and martial arts.

Tickets are $18.00, $25.00 and $30.00 and group discounts are available for groups of 15 or more. Tickets can be purchased through Ticketmaster (www.ticketmaster.ca) or the CCC ((604) 687-0729). For group orders contact the CCC for a group ticket order form.
