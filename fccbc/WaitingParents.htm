Waiting for a Child from China?

FCC is committed to helping adopting families right from the moment they submit their application. It can be a very long wait from the time you complete all the formalities with your agency and the time you finally travel to China and pick up your long awaited child. Our experience is that the waiting families group can be a tremendous boon in helping to bridge the gap.
