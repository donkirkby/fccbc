CCAA Delegation Visit

By Peter Gibson, FCC BC & NWWA

One could not have asked for a better turn out, especially on short notice. About 30 or so families attended a buffet dinner at Grand Buffet (6401 Kingsway, Burnaby) on Wednesday evening November 17, 1999 to meet five representatives of the Central Chinese Adoption Agency (CCAA) and 2 Social Welfare Institutes.

The delegation, lead by Mr. Zhang, Zhong, Deputy Director of CCAA, met with families at the end of a visit to the US and Canada prior to heading back to China. Other members of the delegation included Ms. Zhao, L. and Mr. Xing, K. of the CCAA, Ms. Wang, C. of the Beijing Social Welfare Institute and Mr. Chang, Q. of Yueyang Social Welfare Institute.

The evening was informal and we occupied half of the restaurant. David Robinson formally welcomed the delegates on behalf of the families, which was followed by a Chinese song sung by some of the children. Mr. Zhang extended his greetings to the group after which there were opportunities for the children to have pictures taken with the delegates.

Many thanks to Bonnie Wong for arranging the dinner and coordinating the evening. She did an excellent job getting the word out regarding the dinner. Now if we could somehow get as good a turn out for the AGM....
