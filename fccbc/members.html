Membership

Family membership in FCC BC is $28 per calendar year. Membership is free for first-time waiting families while they wait. Please select the appropriate item when you register.

Members receive:

  • Information on upcoming events via newsletter and email
  • FCC BC newsletter, Ni Hao is mailed two times per year
  • Discount at FCC events
  • Opportunity to join an online discussion group as well as participate in influencing the priorities and directions of the organization.
  • Opportunity to apply for regional grants to support FCCBC activities throughout the province.

To become a member or renew your membership, use the Munch a Lunch service.

If you already have a munch a lunch account (for your child's school) please send an email to Munch A Lunch with the subject line "Need 2 accounts - one for XYZ school and one for FCC BC" and they will set up 2 separate accounts for you.

If you'd like more details about using Munch a Lunch, download our user guide (PDF).
