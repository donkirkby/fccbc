Adoption Sites
Adoptive Parents Association of British Columbia Our local support group for all adoption information. The best source of info on local regulations, agencies, and seminars.
Main FCC Site The parent of our own organization.
China Adoption Resource Guide. An interesting site with lots of info on China and Chinese Adoptions. American content and interests.
China Adoption Centre. This site is in China, maintained by the Chinese government departments responsible for managing adoptions in China. In both English and Chinese; to see the Chinese characters, you'll need a chinese character set loaded into your browser.
Our Chinese Daughter's Foundation. American site dedicated to helping single women adopt from China. Also has a lot of great info on China and Chinese Adoptions.
Coming Home/Shared Blessings. Information on Adoptions, and a newsletter devoted to Adoptions and China News.
Child of My Dreams. Information about adoption, international or domestic, and support and information on dealing with infertility.
China Sprout. A fun site that contains lots of cultural stuff for sail, such as music, clothing and art, and resources on China adoption.
ARIA - Adoption Research Organization - Lots of studies and info on Adoption.
Adoption Research papers on ARIA's site
Personal China Adoption Stories is exactly that - people's stories and photos posted on the net. Heartening if you're waiting, or even just thinking about it.

Lonely Planets China Guide. Lots of info and a cool map.
