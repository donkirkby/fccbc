What is the FCC?

FCC BC is an affiliation of families in British Columbia with children adopted in China. FCC BC is in turn, affiliated with FCCs in other parts of North America and elsewhere and with the Adoptive Families Association of BC (AFA BC).

Our mission is to provide a community resource for families who have adopted children from China by:

  • Providing a peer group for parents and children, to let them know they're not alone
  • Scheduling events reflecting the culture of our children's birthplace
  • Providing information on adoption and Chinese adoption specifically to our members and anyone else interested in adopting in China
  • Helping to educate our members and the public on the experience and issues of adopting Children from China
  • Supporting fundraising to benefit children in orphanages in China
  • Celebrate our families

We help make the path from investigating the possibilities to raising an adoptive family of mixed race a great joy.
