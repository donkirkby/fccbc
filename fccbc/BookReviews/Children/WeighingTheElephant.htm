Weighing the Elephant

By Ting-xing Ye, art by Suzane Langlois, published by Annick Press Ltd.

Reviewed by Heather McDonald, FCC BC & NW WA

This is a beautiful story about how a seven-year old boy, through creativity and intelligence, outwits a cruel emperor in order to protect a beloved village mascot.

The challenge posed by the evil emperor to the village is to provide the exact weight of a baby elephant, or to forfeit the talented animal to the emperor in two days' time. The book has wonderful illustrations and the story is believable. In fact, your child will have the advantage of an interesting story and science lesson rolled into one! Age Group: 3 through 8
