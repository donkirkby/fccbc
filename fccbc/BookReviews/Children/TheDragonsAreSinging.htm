The Dragons are Singing Tonight

By Jack Prelutsky, illustrations by Peter Sis, published by Mulberry Books; William Morrow & Co. New York 1993

Reviewed by Heather McDonald, FCC BC & NWWA

This would be a good choice for a Chinese New Year's gift for a child. It is not about Chinese New Year, but is a book devoted to poetry with dragon themes. It was a New York Times Notable Book of the Year. The poetry is Jack Prelutsky at his best, with titles such as "I'm an Amiable Dragon"; "I Wish I had a Dragon"; "The Dragons are Singing Tonight", and my personal favorite "I am Boom! The Thunder Dragon". The artwork is superb. The rhymes are a good introduction to poetry, and soon you will find that your child knows most of the poems by heart. This is particularly true when they begin running around the house shouting "I am Boom! The Thunder Dragon, taller than the tallest trees…" Ages 3-10
