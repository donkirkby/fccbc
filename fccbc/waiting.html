Waiting Families

From the moment you commit to adopting from China you will be on a voyage of discovery. As much as the internet is a huge source of information, it's still hard to beat meeting face to face with families who have already walked the path of adoption and also to network with others in your area at the same stage as you in the process.

We are committed to providing Waiting Families with a forum to meet and network with others. We provide a pleasant, informal and friendly atmosphere for you to meet, and will do our best to provide answers to your questions and to help you meet others at the same stage of the process as you.

If you would like to attend a waiting families event or if you'd like more information on the program please contact , FCC BC Waiting Families Coordinator.
